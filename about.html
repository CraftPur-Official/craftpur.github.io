    Know more about craftpur!

    First of all we would like to thank you all, those who are reading and those who are supporting the content. We are an Indian Minecraft Community which is dedicated to deliver the best content to it's viewer.

    craftpur was founded on July 13th, 2018, started as a get together event druing the lockdown which was faced by the world, over time people loved the series and demanded more seasons. In view of of this demand, we are bringing back some seasons.

    CraftPur is a group of minecraft players who just love to have fun together in minecraft . We are just pocket edition players right now but we plan to further expand the platform. The first two seasons that is Season1 and Season2 we just the trial seasons. But the third season Season3 was successfully launched on Arpil 24, 2021.

    If you are an official CraftPur Member then these rules are for you!
